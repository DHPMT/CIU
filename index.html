Company Profile

☕ Where does your money come from?

(Spoiler: It's all because of YOU!)

📖 Book lovers pay us to share their books

Coffee writers and publishers around the world want YOU to read their books. They pay us to advertise — and we give that money right back to you. So every time you read, someone out there is paying us to thank you!

🌱 Your time is valuable

You're learning real coffee skills while earning. That's a win-win. We believe knowledge should never go unrewarded.

💰 Why do people deposit?

When you upgrade to D levels, you're not "paying us" — you're unlocking higher earnings. Think of it like buying better farming tools:

  • Intern = hand hoe (small harvest)
  • D1-D9 = tractors & machines (bigger harvest)

Your deposit sits in your wallet and works for you. Plus, when you upgrade, previous level cost is refunded. You're always in control.

👥 Where do deposits go?

Your deposits help us:

  • Pay our team (developers, support staff, community managers) who keep this platform running for you 24/7
  • Rent our office in Milan, Italy where we coordinate everything
  • Fund farmer support programs (tools, seedlings, training)
  • Pay book authors so they keep writing more coffee books for you to read and earn from
  • Expand to Uganda — your deposits are paving the way for our local office!

Every deposit helps build something bigger than all of us.

🤝 You bring friends, we share the love

When your friends join and read, the whole community grows. We share a little of what we earn with you — because you helped make it happen.

🇺🇬 Our Big Dream

We want to reach 10 million people in Uganda. Why? Because when we do, we'll open a UIC office right here in Uganda — with jobs, training centers, and direct support for coffee farmers. Every read gets us closer.

☕ Coffee partners believe in you

Coffee farms, exporters, and agricultural organizations support this platform because they want to see a new generation of coffee champions rise. Their support helps keep the rewards flowing.

💚 A little goes back to the farm

A small part helps us run things and support farmer programs — like giving tools and seedlings to real coffee farmers. So every read helps someone, somewhere.

🛠️ Farmer Support Program

We help coffee farmers access essential tools and equipment through our community support program:

  • Nursery: Polythene pots, Hand sprayers, Watering cans, Shade nets
  • Field Maintenance: Secateurs, Pruning saws, Spray pumps, Spades, Wheelbarrows
  • Harvesting: Harvesting baskets, Tarpaulins, Moisture testers
  • Processing: Coffee pulpers, Fermentation tanks, Drying beds
  • Milling & Grading: Hullers, Gravity separators, Color sorters
